YOUR EXPERTS SINCE 2021


Since 2021, Red Paws has been working towards rescuing old and abandoned dogs, and providing permanent homes to neglected or abused dogs.

You can drop by - even without prior appointment - to help volunteer. Volunteering includes feeding the dogs and spending time with traumatized dogs(that includes cuddling).

Unfortunately, fear and ignorance have led many Indian citizens and officials to see street dogs as nothing but a nuisance and to seek eradication using inhumane methods such as poisoning and beating.

Our main goal is to make people aware and erradicate the ongoing abuse to dogs.Through our programs, all the dogs within a community or region are sterilized and then released back to their territories.
The result: dog breeding stops and their population declines.

All of our dogs are Spayed/Neutered, Microchipped, Vaccinated, De-wormed, and HW/FIV tested and Prevention Given prior to adoption. Adoption fees cover these vetting expenses.
Puppies receive 3 Distemper/Parvo shots and other vetting once they reach 6 months old.
